Background Size

  • Length - width and height - set just one and the other is auto.
  • Percentage - Sets the H and W as percent of parent.
  • Cover - scale to smallest size that W and H BOTH fit in parent.
  • Contain - scale to Largest size that W and H BOTH fit in parent.
