The trouble with IDs

Specificity.

You have a list and need to show an alert on one item in the list.

#featured li {color:#369;} #featured li.alert {color:red;} .featured li {color:#369;} li.alert {color:red;}

#2 is better - you can use it anywhere. In fact...

.featured li {color:#369;} .alert {color:red !important;}

I know - Somewhere Jeffrey Zeldman and Eric Meyer just got chills. A puppy may have even met it's end.
